Joining the orchestra as a musician:

Please send an email to : Personnel@RidgewoodSymphony.org

Please put "PERSONNEL" in the subject line. Include your contact information and a brief resume of your musical education and experiences.

 

Joining the Friends of the Ridgewood Symphony (FRSO):

The FRSO members spearhead a multitude of projects at concerts, Project Symphony, Festival Strings, fund raising events, and in their communities, all in support of the Ridgewood Symphony Orchestra.

If you wish to learn more about volunteering to support these and other rewarding RSO programs, please send an e-mail to General.Manager@ridgewoodsymphony.org (please put "FRSO" or "VOLUNTEER" in the subject line) or by telephone at 201-612-0118.
