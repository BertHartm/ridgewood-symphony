Outreach

Ridgewood Symphony Orchestra Festival Strings

An Educational Outreach Program

Festival Strings is an educational outreach program sponsored by the Ridgewood Symphony Orchestra, and includes more than 100 young musicians from over 30 towns in northern New Jersey. The group comes together for five intense rehearsals and a wonderful concert in Ridgewood each winter.
